Subway System Design

An urban planner is trying to design a new subway system. They have represented their design with a diagram, where:

  • Subway stations are represented by graph nodes.
  • Train connections between stations are represented by edges labeled next. These connections are strictly one-way.
    • A next edge from Station A to Station B means that there is a a train that goes directly from from Station A to Station B.
    • If there is nonext edge from Station B to Station A, there is no direct train from Station B to Station A.
  • The relative positions of stations are represented by edges labelled north and east.
    • A north edge means that the pointed at station is directly north of the pointing station.
    • An east edge means that the pointed at station is directly east of the pointing station.
