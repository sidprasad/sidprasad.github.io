Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a game is played on a 3x3 grid, but two players : X and O. Players take turns placing their mark in an empty cell, and the first player to get three of their marks in a row (either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins. The diagram below represents a game of Tic Tac Toe, being played on a 3 x 3 grid. Each cell is represented by a graph node, and each edge represents a relationship between two cells.

  • For instance, if an edge labelled right points from Cell1 to Cell2, this means that Cell2 is directly to the right of Cell1.
  • Similarly, if an edge labelled down points from Cell1 to Cell2, this means that Cell2 is directly below Cell1.
Finally, if a mark has been placed in a cell, the cell will have an attribute mark with a value of either X or O.
