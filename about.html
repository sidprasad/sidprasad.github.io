I grew up in New Delhi, India and recently graduated from Tufts University. I'm about to start work as a Software Engineer in the Windows and Devices Group at Microsoft in Redmond, WA. I am interested in programming languages, especially the application of Domain Specific Languages to systems problems. This has led to some work in compilers and parsing.

I play the guitar and love jazz. Until very recently, I played for the Tufts University Small Jazz Ensemble. I also (infrequently) write a blog on my thoughts on jazz-based musicology and theory. I played tennis as a kid, and watch a lot of sports. I'm a huge Arsenal fan, and (almost) never miss a match.
